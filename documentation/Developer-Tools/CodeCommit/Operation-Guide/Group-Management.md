**新建群组**

进入群组页面，点击“新建群组”

![1.png](https://github.com/jdcloudcom/cn/blob/edit/image/codecommit/Group1.png)


填写群组名称、描述，并进行权限设置

![2.png](https://github.com/jdcloudcom/cn/blob/edit/image/codecommit/Group2.png)

点击Create group
